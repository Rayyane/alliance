About Alliance

Alliance Pediatrics' primary goal is to provide high quality, evidence-based treatments and training opportunities to our clients. We believe individualized attention and a collaborative relationship are necessary components of solution-based care.

Our aim is to create a trusting, reliable and caring environment for our patients and their families. We strive to provide our clients with the skills they need to continue to grow and develop personally and professionally.

Our family services include the assessment of presenting concerns and a comprehensive treatment plan to address identified issues. Treatment plans are always tailored to meet the needs of the client. However they frequently include individual therapy for the child as well as caregiver training. In addition, we provide families with oversight of home therapy programs, school consultations and collaboration with other care-providers.

Currently, we serve many different clients. Some of our clients include children, adolescents, caregivers, related service professionals (occupational therapists, physical therapists, speech-language pathologists, psychologists, etc), therapists seeking professional development (BCBA certification, psychology licensure) and school staff.

We work with professionals in a variety of ways. Some of these services include training seminars for direct care staff and school personnel, clinical case consultation and supervision opportunities.
